Providing Personalized and High Quality Services

AL-Falah Group Human Resources Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1997 A.D. and started its recruitment operation with an office comprising of only few staff. Since then, we have been able to set up a bench mark in our business scope with our best services provided by dedicated and dynamic staff members. Having been awarded with letter of appreciation from Ministry of Labor clients & social organizations we have achieved the best of all We are the first in the country of its kind to be certified with ISO 9001-2008 in the year 2004 as an outcome of our qualitative management system. We are continuously adapting latest technology of working pattern in office to increase efficiency of the work load so as to meet the actual requirement of our prominent multinational clients which has reached up to hundred in number.

We also focus to support national priorities to reduce unemployment and generate foreign remittance thereby raising the socio-economic status of underprivileged people of Nepal.It has always been a part of our culture to concentrate and perceive the requirements of our valued clients in order to execute them within a given deadline. We heartily welcome any suggestions and look forward to a long lasting business relationship.

We remain thankful to every supportive ones.
