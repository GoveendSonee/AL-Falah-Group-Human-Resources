Vision

To grow beyond the horizon of goals and deliver quality services continuously in order to become a hallmark of a dynamic organization responding to the clients needs.

Welcome to AL-Falah Group Human Resources

AL-Falah A ManPower Company

We're in this business since 2079 and We provide the best human resoure services

AL-Falah Group Human Resources Pvt. Ltd., (established in 2079) started its recruitment operation with an office comprising of only few staff. Since then, we have been able to set up a bench mark in our business scope with our best professional services which is effectively maintained by dedicated and dynamic staff members. Having been awarded with letter of appreciation from Ministry of Labor, clients & social organizations, we achieved the best of all when first in the country of its kind to be certified with ISO 9001-2008 in the year 2004 as an outcome of our qualitative management system.
We are continuously adopting latest technology of working pattern in office to increase efficiency of the work load so as to meet the actual requirement of our prominent multinational clients which has reached up to hundred in numbers.
